Brioche

Ingredients

  • 360g of flour
  • 200g of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 15cl of milk
  • 30g of sugar
  • 8g of dry yeast
  • 7g of salt

Recipy

  1. In a small recipient, mix 60g of flour, 15cl of room temperature milk, the yeast and a pinch of sugar. Let it rest for 15 minutes until the yeast is blooming
  2. In a bigger bowl, mix the flour, the eggs, the sugar and the salt. Incorporate the butter in small chunk while kneading.
  3. Incorporate the yeast mixture and knead until a smooth and elastic doughball is formed.
  4. Let it rise for 2-3 hours at room temperature, or overnight in the fridge.
  5. Press on the dough to let out the air, cut it in 4-5 equal pieces.
  6. Form the "tresse" and let it rise for 2 more hours.
  7. In a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 25 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for an hour. Enjoy.
